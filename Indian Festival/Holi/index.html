Festival of Colors

About Holi

Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated with joy and enthusiasm across India and many parts of the world. It marks the arrival of spring, the victory of good over evil, and the joy of new beginnings. People throw colored powders, dance, sing, and celebrate with family and friends.

How People Celebrate

Throwing Colors

People gather to throw vibrant colors at each other, filling the streets with excitement.

Music and Dance

Traditional songs and dances accompany the celebrations as everyone joins in the festivities.

Bonfire Ritual

A bonfire is lit on the eve of Holi to symbolize the burning of evil and the triumph of good.
