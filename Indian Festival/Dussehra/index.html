Celebrating Dussehra

Victory of Good over Evil

About Dussehra

Dussehra is a Hindu festival that celebrates the victory of good over evil. It marks the defeat of the demon king Ravana by Lord Rama. People celebrate by burning effigies of Ravana, performing prayers, and engaging in cultural performances.

Celebration Around the World

Ravana Burning

On Dussehra, effigies of Ravana are set on fire, symbolizing the destruction of evil.

Dandiya Dance

Traditional dance performances with sticks are a highlight of the celebrations.

Ram Lila

The story of Lord Rama's victory is enacted through dramatic performances.
