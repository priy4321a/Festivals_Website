About Raksha Bandhan

Raksha Bandhan is a festival that celebrates the bond of love between brothers and sisters. On this day, sisters tie a sacred thread (rakhi) around their brother's wrist, and in return, brothers vow to protect them from harm. The festival symbolizes the deep connection and love shared between siblings.
