Happy Ram Navami!

Celebrating the Birth of Lord Ram

About Ram Navami

Ram Navami is a Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ram, the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It falls on the ninth day of Chaitra month in the Hindu calendar and is celebrated with great zeal and devotion across India. People visit temples, sing devotional songs, and hold processions to mark the occasion.

How People Celebrate

Temple Visits

Devotees visit temples to offer prayers and participate in special ceremonies and rituals on this auspicious day.

Ram Katha

Special readings of the Ramayana (Ram Katha) are held, narrating the life and deeds of Lord Ram.

Grand Processions

People participate in grand processions, singing devotional hymns and celebrating the divine presence of Lord Ram.
